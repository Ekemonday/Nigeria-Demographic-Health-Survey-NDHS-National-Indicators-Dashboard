# Nigeria-Demographic-Health-Survey-NDHS-National-Indicators-Dashboard

The Nigeria Demographic and Health Survey (NDHS) is a nationally representative survey conducted periodically to collect detailed information on population, health, and nutrition across Nigeria. It serves as a critical data source for policymakers, researchers, development partners, and public health practitioners in tracking progress toward national and global health goals.

🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard
This dashboard is designed to provide a clear, visual, and interactive summary of key demographic and health indicators derived from the NDHS dataset. It enables users to:

Monitor health trends across states and geopolitical zones
Identify regional disparities in health outcomes and service utilization
Track progress on key health indicators such as maternal health, child nutrition, immunization, and family planning
Support evidence-based decision making for health interventions and policy formulation
