# Nigeria-Demographic-Health-Survey-NDHS-National-Indicators-Dashboard

The Nigeria Demographic and Health Survey (NDHS) is a nationally representative survey conducted periodically to collect detailed information on population, health, and nutrition across Nigeria. It serves as a critical data source for policymakers, researchers, development partners, and public health practitioners in tracking progress toward national and global health goals.
